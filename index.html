SafeDrive Alert

A Drowsiness Detection App for Drivers

  1. Speed detection : Alert the driver when they exceed the speed limit.
  2. Distraction Detection : Using computer vision techniques to detect when the driver is distracted, such as by using a mobile phone, and provide alerts to refocus their attention on the road.
  3. Emergency Assistance : Implementing a feature that automatically contacts emergency services or designated contacts in case of a detected accident or emergency situation.
  4. Personalized Settings : Allow users to customize alert thresholds, sensitivity levels, and preferences based on their driving habits and preferences.
  5. Real-time Traffic Updates : Providing real-time updates on traffic conditions, road closures, and alternative routes to help drivers plan their journeys more efficiently.
